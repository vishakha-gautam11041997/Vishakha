Our Interest



Cell Biology & Yeast Biology: Understanding cellular processes, gene regulation, and metabolic pathways using yeast as a model system.

Cheminformatics: Developing computational approaches for molecular activity prediction, drug discovery, and structure-activity relationship (SAR) modeling.


Computer Vision for Biological Image Analysis: Applying deep learning and image processing techniques for microscopy image analysis, cell segmentation, and phenotypic profiling.


Single-Cell Genomics: Investigating cellular heterogeneity and gene expression dynamics at the single-cell level using multi-omics approaches.
